Personality Profile

Sun in Leo · 8th House The Core

You radiate natural authority and creativity but channel it inward rather than outwardly showboating. The 8th house gives your Leo sun a depth and intensity that is unusual — you are fascinated by transformation, hidden dynamics and the mechanics of power. You pursue recognition but on your own terms and through mastery rather than performance.

Moon in Capricorn · 1st/2nd House The Inner Life

Emotionally you are reserved and self-reliant. You feel things deeply but rarely let it show until trust is fully established. There is a strong drive to build security — financial, emotional and material — and you tend to process feelings through productivity. You can come across as composed or even stoic in difficult moments.

Sagittarius Rising · Neptune Conjunct ASC The Outer Persona

To the world you project optimism, adventure and philosophical breadth. Neptune conjunct your Ascendant adds a layer of idealism and mystique — people sense something bigger than ordinary about you. You likely come across as someone searching for meaning and inspiring others to do the same.

Mars, Saturn & Pluto in Libra · 10th House Ambition & Career

Three heavy planets in your 10th house tells a story of someone who works methodically and strategically toward long-term goals. Mars and Saturn together produce someone who delays action until conditions are right — and then executes with precision. Pluto here signals the potential for significant impact or transformation in your professional sphere.

Venus in Cancer · 7th House Relationships

In partnership you are deeply nurturing and loyal. You take time to open up but once committed you are all-in. You are attracted to emotionally intelligent partners and function best in relationships that feel like a safe home base.

Sun Square Moon The Shadow

The tension between your Leo solar desire for recognition and your Capricorn moon's emotional restraint creates an internal push-pull — wanting warmth and connection but defaulting to distance and control when vulnerable. Working through this integration is a lifelong theme.
